Important Note on Suffixes and Prefixes (e.g., MD):

"Where a name includes a suffix (IlI, Jr.), we do not use a comma to separate it from the name, regardless of how it appears on screen. It is treated as an integral part of the surname, i.e. Downey Jr., Robert. The same applies to the corresponding suffixes in foreign languages, like pÃ¨re, fils, hijo, etc. Courtesy titles like Mr., Dr., Sir, and degree indications like Ph.D., are not considered part of the name, and are not included in the primary name, even if that is how the name appears on screen. They are included as alternate names. (Exception: In the silent era, it was not unusual for women to be credited as, i.e., Mrs. John Smith -- in such cases, Mrs. is an integral part of the name, since John Smith is an entirely different person)" - IMDb.

For individuals like Hari, while IMDb does not allow suffixes (e.g., MD) attached directly to the primary name on the poster/IMDb page, your name is listed as requested in the film's credits. Having a personal IMDb Pro profile with your picture will help distinguish your name from similar ones.

Project Overview

Logline: A lonely doll maker accidentally brings his favorite doll to life, only to discover the terrifying truth behind its sudden animation.

Synopsis: In a quaint, forgotten corner of the city, an aging and reclusive doll maker, haunted by his past, crafts a masterpiece named "Bella." One stormy night, a strange electrical surge brings Bella to life, fulfilling the doll maker's deepest desires for companionship. However, Bella's animated form harbors a sinister secret, as the source of her life begins to mimic and consume the essence of those around her, forcing the doll maker to confront the monstrous reality of his creation.
